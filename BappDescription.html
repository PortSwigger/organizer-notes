Organizer Notes is a Burp Suite extension designed to streamline your penetration testing process by simplifying note-taking. It enhances your workflow by automatically attaching selected notes to HTTP requests that you save in the Organizer.

Features

This extension is designed to integrate smoothly into your pentesting workflow. As a result, it includes the following features:

  • Automatically attaches selected notes to saved HTTP requests in the Organizer.
  • Offers a seamless note-taking process to enhance efficiency during pentesting.

Usage

Using Organizer Notes is straightforward:

  1. While in the process of testing, make your notes as you would normally.
  2. When saving HTTP requests in the Organizer, the extension will automatically attach your notes to the designated request.

This extension simplifies the task of keeping track of important insights and findings during a pentest by keeping your notes and HTTP requests tightly linked.
